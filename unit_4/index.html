Camilla Shawl

From Madder by Carrie Bostick Hodge. In Bumblebirch Cocoon - a 2ply lace weight in silk and merino on size 3 needles. Cast on additional repeats of the shell pattern to make up for the smaller gauge. This was a very fun pattern to knit. It was well written and the lace pattern easy to memorize.

Salish Toque by Silvia Olsen. Knit in Custom Woolen Mills Ltd Prairie Wool Lopi Soft Spun. This hat was started kit from a class with Silvia Olsen about knitting in the Cowichan style of catching floats. A great technique and I need to knit more from her Knitting with Wool book.

Socks

These socks were knit for my husband during Sock Madness 2009 (!). This is most definitely a favorite pattern. Knit in Knit Picks Dancing and Risata - these yarns have really held up well as Gary wears them often. Knit on size 0 needles at 9 1/2 stitches per inch - the tight gauge really helps the longevity of socks.
